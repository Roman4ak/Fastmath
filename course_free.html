Course Description

  • Get an introduction to the FastMath calculation methods, improve your mental math, and get a sample of the calculations required in Case Interviews and business job interviews at elite Consulting, Finance, Tech, and CPG firms.
  • The FastMath Free Interview Calculation Methods contains the basic math methods to get you up and running with mental math preparation for Case Interview and business job interview calculations.
  • Recommended for candidates of all backgrounds that are looking for a place to start training their mental math skills or improve their chances in both Case Interviews, business job interviews, or any tests that require performing calculations.
