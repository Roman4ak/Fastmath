Benefits of Becoming a Verified FASTMATH User

  • Becoming a Verified FastMath User is FREE.
  • Verified FastMath Users gain access to exclusive free FastMath resources, including business math and interview preparation resources.
  • Verified FastMath Users are pre-approved to enroll in the FastMath Advanced Case Calculations Methods Online Course. To access this exclusive course, it is required that users have become Verified FastMath Users.

Information Required to Become a Verified FASTMATH User

  • General personal information, including legal name, citizenship, and country of residence, and LinkedIn profile URL
  • Identity Verification Information (at least one of the following):
    • A University email address (either student or alumni) to verify affiliation with a university. We will verify this email address.
