What is FastMath?

Different FastMath courses cover different topics and needs, ranging from students that want to just practice their calculation skills, to professionals preparing for some of the world’s most difficult interview calculations, like McKinsey’s final round interviews.

The FastMath Interview Calculation Essentials course covers the calculation skills I believe are essential in today’s business jobs interview, in order to differentiate from other job candidates and succeed in any business job interview. FastMath Interview Calculation Essentials methods include Breakeven, Profitability, Market Sizing, and NPV calculations.
